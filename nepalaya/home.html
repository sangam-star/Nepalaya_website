Welcome to Nepalaya School/College

NEPALAYA College was established in 1999 A.D., during the early development of the modern higher secondary education system in Nepal. Our goal was to offer globally accepted education to local learners. Since then, we have become one of Nepal's prominent educational institutions, steadily transforming into a global learning center.

About Us

Nepalaya College is committed to providing quality education and fostering a learning environment that encourages innovation and excellence. Our mission is to nurture students into global citizens with strong ethical values.
