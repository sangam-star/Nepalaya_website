TRIBHUVAN UNIVERSITY (TU)

Nepalaya is an established private educational institute. All undergraduate and master's programs are affiliated with Tribhuvan University. Nepalaya offers BSc CSIT, BBM, BCA, BBS, and MBS programs.

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

This program equips students with a strong foundation in computer applications and software development, preparing them for careers in IT and software engineering.

Bachelor of Science in CSIT (BSc CSIT)

This program offers deep knowledge in computer science, programming, data structures, and IT management, preparing students for tech roles in various sectors.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The BBA program develops leadership and business management skills across finance, marketing, HR, and entrepreneurship.

Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

BBS focuses on comprehensive business education including accounting, economics, and organizational behavior for careers in finance and administration.

Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM)

The BHM program offers training in hotel operations and customer service, preparing graduates for global careers in hospitality and tourism.
