ABOUT US

Nepalaya College is an educational institution that provides quality education and fosters academic excellence. It is dedicated to nurturing students with the skills and knowledge required for their personal and professional growth.

Nepalaya College was established in 1999 A.D., in the preliminary age of the modern higher secondary education system in Nepal with the purpose of offering globally accepted education to the learners in their own local area. We have been one of the prominent educational institutions in Nepal since then. As a result of our continuous and dedicated effort, presently, we are going to be a global learning center for creating global opportunities for all the learners offering distinct courses to the students in four different buildings with nearly eighty well-organized classrooms.

We believe in equality with diversity. Nepalaya is affiliated with Tribhuvan University (TU) and National Examinations Board (NEB) to offer various courses and degrees under different levels. We have various specialized and elective courses to cater to the needs and demands of the students in different levels of study starting from Pre-school programs, Higher Secondary Programs - Science, Management, Humanities, Law, and Undergraduate Programs.

Lab and Sports at Our College

Nepalaya College is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratory facilities that provide students with hands-on experience in various scientific and technical fields. The labs are well-maintained and designed to foster innovation and practical learning. From computer labs with the latest technology to science labs with modern equipment, students have access to resources that enhance their academic journey and prepare them for real-world challenges. The college ensures that all safety measures are in place, creating a secure environment for experimentation and exploration.

Sports play a vital role in the holistic development of students at Nepalaya College. The institution encourages active participation in various sports activities, providing facilities for games like basketball, football, volleyball, and table tennis. The college organizes regular sports events and competitions to promote teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness among students. With a dedicated sports ground and experienced coaches, Nepalaya College ensures that students can balance academics with physical well-being, fostering a spirit of healthy competition and camaraderie.
